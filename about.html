A little bit about me...

My design philosophy is deeply rooted in the principles of sacred geometry, a connection I have nurtured since my teenage years when my brother introduced me to the band Tool. The band’s use of the mathematics of the universe sparked my fascination with intricate patterns and the unity that underlies all things. Wearing a flower of life ring since I was 15 serves as my daily reminder of this interconnectedness, and it reflects in the meticulous detail I bring to my designs.

Whether I’m building interfaces or crafting graphics, I focus on precision and commitment to the smallest elements, ensuring nothing is out of place. My love for travel and hands-on work with woodworking further influences my approach to design, blending structure, creativity, and craft into everything I create. These personal passions shape not only my aesthetic but also the intentionality I bring to my work, resulting in designs that feel both grounded and expansive.
