Method

Atlas's development fully embraced the Goal-Directed Design (GDD) method, a systematic approach crafted by Alan Cooper. GDD was the cornerstone of our design process from inception to completion. Typically, this process starts with stakeholder and kickoff meetings and culminates in the final product. Given the 16-week timeframe of our Spring semester, we adapted GDD to fit our project's timeline.
